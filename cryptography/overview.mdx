> Simplicity is the ultimate form of art. We prioritize simplicity and usability through a multimodal, dynamic design fine-tuning performance and security.

Fairblock avoids one-size-fits-all cryptography. Instead, the right scheme is selected for each use case, balancing performance, security, and usability.

## Core Technologies

| Technology | Abbreviation | Primary Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Distributed Key Generation | DKG | Joint key pair generation without single-party control |
| MPC Identity-Based Encryption | MPC-IBE | Pre-execution confidentiality, sealed-bid auctions |
| Multi-Party Computation | MPC | Collaborative computation without revealing inputs |
| Homomorphic Encryption | HE | Computation over encrypted data (confidential transfers) |
| Zero-Knowledge Proofs | ZKP | Proving statements without revealing underlying data |

## Current Implementation (V1)

Two primary schemes are active:

1. **Lightweight HE**: Twisted ElGamal for confidential transfers. Fast, mobile-friendly, low gas cost.
2. **MPC-IBE**: For pre-execution confidentiality and conditional decryption. Powers frontrunning protection, sealed-bid auctions, limit orders, and selective disclosure.
