## How It Works

Stabletrust uses **Twisted ElGamal** (fast additive homomorphic encryption) combined with lightweight ZK proofs:

1. **Addresses stay transparent** — wallets, compliance tools, and apps see *who*
2. **Amounts are encrypted** — no one sees *how much* unless authorized
3. **Selective disclosure** — auditors can decrypt exactly what policy allows (e.g., "amount of transaction #123" or "balances over a period")

Decryption keys are generated via distributed key generation (DKG) — per-account and per-auditor.
