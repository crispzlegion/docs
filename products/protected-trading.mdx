Protected Trading enables confidential execution on decentralized exchanges — encrypted orders, intents, and bids for better price discovery without centralized intermediaries.

## The Problem

On public DEXs, order flow is fully visible:

- **MEV extraction** — sandwich attacks and front-running drain value from traders
- **Information leakage** — large orders signal intent before execution
- **Adverse selection** — market makers widen spreads to compensate for toxic flow

## Fairblock's Solution

Fairblock encrypts orders at submission time using threshold IBE. Orders remain encrypted in the mempool and are only decrypted after inclusion in a block — eliminating MEV and front-running opportunities.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Encrypt Order">
    The trader encrypts their order (type, amount, price) using FairyRing's Master Public Key. The encrypted order is submitted to the destination chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Block Inclusion">
    The encrypted order is included in a block. Validators and MEV searchers cannot read the order contents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decryption">
    FairyRing validators derive the decryption key for the block height. The order is decrypted and executed at the fair price.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Applications
