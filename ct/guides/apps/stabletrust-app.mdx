Stabletrust is the browser-based interface for confidential stablecoin transfers across multiple blockchain networks.

## Features

- **Wallet integration** with MetaMask, WalletConnect, and any EVM wallet
- **Browser-based encryption** using local, lightweight homomorphic encryption and ZK proofs
- **Balance decryption** visible only to you, privately in-browser
- **Multi-chain support** across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, and more

## No Overhead

Stabletrust requires no volatile privacy tokens, no bridging to isolated chains, no wallet switching, and minimal encryption delays.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Visit [stabletrust.fairblock.network](https://stabletrust.fairblock.network) and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Make a one-time deposit to set up your confidential account on FairyRing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer">
    Send confidential transfers. Amounts and balances are encrypted, visible only to you and the recipient.
  </Step>
</Steps>
