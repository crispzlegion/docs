title: "YieldShield App"
description: "Private onchain yield"
---

YieldShield is a confidential yield app. Deposits earn yield through Morpho vaults, while amounts and balances stay encrypted onchain and remain compliance-ready through selective disclosure.

## Features

- Yield sourced from Morpho vaults, accruing automatically on your balance
- Encrypted balances using Twisted ElGamal homomorphic encryption
- Selective disclosure for regulatory compliance

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get a yield-bearing stablecoin">
    Convert a supported stablecoin into yield-bearing shares. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit into the confidential app">
    Deposit it to receive an encrypted balance visible only to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn passively">
    Your balance accrues yield automatically. No lockup periods and no extra steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transact privately">
    Send and receive while amounts and balances remain encrypted onchain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
