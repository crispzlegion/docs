title: "Private Yield"
description: "Earn yield on confidential balances"
---

Private Yield allows users to earn yield on their confidential balances without exposing positions or strategies onchain.

## Overview

Deposit confidential balances into yield-generating vaults. All amounts, positions, and returns remain encrypted.

## Features

- Encrypted vault deposits and withdrawals
- Hidden yield accrual and compounding
- No exposure of position sizes to MEV bots or competitors
- Selective disclosure for tax reporting and compliance

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Vault">
    Choose a yield vault based on asset type and strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Deposit encrypted balances from your confidential account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn">
    Yield accrues on encrypted balances. Only you can see your returns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Withdraw to your confidential account or directly to a public chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
