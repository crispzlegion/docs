title: "Confidential USD App"
---

Confidential USD (CUSD) is a stablecoin designed for confidential payments, while remaining compliance-ready through selective disclosure controls and traceability. It aims to deliver a digital cash experience for payments and transfers, where amounts and balances are encrypted, without forcing users to rely on volatile privacy coins or isolated privacy chains.

## Features

- Encrypted balances using Twisted ElGamal homomorphic encryption
- Cross-chain deposits and withdrawals
- Selective disclosure for regulatory compliance
- Real-time mobile transaction speeds

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the CUSD App">
    <Card title="Launch CUSD App" href="https://fairblock-cusd-app.vercel.app/">
      Open the Confidential USD application in your browser to get started.
    </Card>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Connect any supported browser wallet to get started. MetaMask, Rainbow, and other injected wallets are all compatible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get CUSD from Any Supported Stablecoin">
    Deposit any supported stablecoin (USDC, USDT) to receive CUSD.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transact Privately">
    Send, receive, and manage CUSD within the safe interface. All transaction amounts and balances remain encrypted onchain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
