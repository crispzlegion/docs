## EVM Chains

Precompiled contracts and escrow contracts serve as bridge endpoints. Tokens lock on EVM, encrypted balances appear on FairyRing.

**Supported:** Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Hyperliquid, and any EVM-compatible chain.

| Component | Role |
| --- | --- |
| **Escrow Contract** | Locks tokens on the source EVM chain |
| **IBC Relayer** | Communicates between EVM and FairyRing |
| **FairyRing Module** | Manages encrypted balances and ZK verification |

## Cosmos Chains

Native IBC integration. FairyKit modules plug directly into the chain's module system with light-client verification.

## Solana

Cross-chain messaging layer connecting Solana programs with FairyRing's confidential computing.

## Payment Networks

Direct integrations with payment-focused chains including Tempo, Arc, Stable, Plasma, and Noble.
