title: "Deposit, Transfer and Withdraw"
description: "Core operations for confidential transfers"
---

## Deposit

Tokens lock in escrow contracts on the source chain. An encrypted balance is created on FairyRing, visible only to the account owner.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Approve">
    Approve the escrow contract to spend your ERC20 tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lock">
    Tokens lock in the escrow contract on the source chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Encrypt">
    FairyRing creates an encrypted balance using Twisted ElGamal encryption.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confidential Transfer

FairyRing performs homomorphic operations on encrypted balances. The ZK proof ensures the transfer is valid without revealing amounts.

| Step | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| **Encrypt amount** | Sender encrypts the transfer amount locally |
| **Generate proof** | Lightweight ZK proof for authorization and range checks |
| **Submit** | Encrypted transaction submitted to FairyRing |
| **Verify** | Chain verifies the proof without decrypting |
| **Update balances** | Homomorphic subtraction from sender, addition to receiver |

## Withdrawal

Users prove balance ownership, FairyRing burns the encrypted balance, and tokens unlock on the destination chain via IBC.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request">
    Submit a withdrawal request with a ZK proof of balance ownership.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Burn">
    FairyRing verifies the proof and burns the encrypted balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock">
    Tokens unlock from the escrow contract on the destination chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>
