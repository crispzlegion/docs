Fairblock implements confidential-by-default finance. Amounts and balances are encrypted while addresses stay transparent.

## The Problem

Public blockchains expose payment amounts, wallet balances, transaction timing, and trading strategies. This creates front-running vulnerabilities, competitive intelligence leaks, whale targeting, and compliance friction.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Lock tokens on any supported chain. Encrypted balances appear on FairyRing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer">
    Send encrypted amounts using homomorphic operations and ZK proofs. Neither the amount nor the balance is revealed onchain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Redeem confidential balances back to native tokens on your chosen chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Comparison

| Feature | Fairblock | Traditional Privacy |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Interoperability** | Works across existing chains | Requires dedicated privacy chains |
| **Gas Efficiency** | Lower costs than ZK or FHE | High computational overhead |
| **Speed** | Real-time mobile transactions | Limited by proof generation |
| **Compliance** | Selective disclosure for auditors | All-or-nothing privacy |
