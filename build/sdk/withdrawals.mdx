---
title: "Withdrawals"
description: "Redeem confidential balances back to public tokens"
---

Withdraw funds from the confidential layer back to public ERC20 tokens on any supported chain.

## Basic Withdrawal

```typescript
const tx = await client.withdraw({
  token: "USDC",
  amount: "500",
  destinationChain: "ethereum-sepolia", // Optional: defaults to deposit chain
});

console.log("Withdrawal tx:", tx.hash);
```

## Withdrawal Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prove">
    The SDK generates a ZK proof that you own the encrypted balance and the withdrawal amount is valid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Burn">
    The encrypted balance is reduced on FairyRing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Relay">
    An IBC packet is sent to the destination chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock">
    The escrow contract releases the tokens to your wallet on the destination chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cross-Chain Withdrawals

You can withdraw to a different chain than where you deposited:

```typescript
// Deposited on Ethereum, withdraw on Arbitrum
await client.withdraw({
  token: "USDC",
  amount: "500",
  destinationChain: "arbitrum-sepolia",
});
```

<Note>
  Cross-chain withdrawals may take longer due to IBC relay times between chains.
</Note>
