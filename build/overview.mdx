## What You Can Build

Tutorials emphasize MPC and IBE applications:

- **Frontrunning protection**: encrypt transactions until block inclusion
- **Sealed-bid auctions**: confidential bidding with simultaneous reveal
- **Prediction markets**: private predictions with fair settlement
- **Limit orders**: hidden limit orders immune to MEV
- **Selective disclosure**: controlled data access for compliance
- **Access control**: condition-based decryption for gated content

## Confidential Transfers

Confidential stablecoin transfers use Homomorphic Encryption and require **no special integration from developers**: everything is handled automatically by Fairblock's smart contracts on each supported chain.
