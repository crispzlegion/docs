Fairblock provides end-to-end confidential computing for blockchains. Amounts and balances are encrypted while addresses stay transparent, enabling compliance-friendly privacy across DeFi, payments, and institutional finance.

The network operates as a decentralized cryptographic computer built on [FairyRing](/protocol/fairyring), with native support for EVM chains, Cosmos, Solana, and major payment networks.
