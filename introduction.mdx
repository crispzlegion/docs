## The Problem

Open finance cannot scale if every payment, trade, and treasury move is exposed onchain. Public blockchains leak sensitive financial data — payment amounts, wallet balances, timing, and trading strategies — creating risks across the board:

- **Competitive intelligence exposure** — rivals see your treasury moves in real time
- **Front-running vulnerabilities** — MEV bots exploit visible order flow
- **Targeting of high-value wallets** — whale watching enables social engineering and theft
- **Regulatory friction** — full transparency conflicts with data protection requirements

## The Solution

Fairblock provides end-to-end applications that eliminate information leakage, enable fair price discovery, and unlock new use cases for retail, institutions, and businesses.

At its core, Fairblock operates as **a dynamic, decentralized cryptographic computer** with its own execution layer purpose-built for confidential computing. Apps run on [FairyRing](/protocol/fairyring) and integrate across major ecosystems.
