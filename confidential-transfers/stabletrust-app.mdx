Stabletrust is the unified browser-based interface for users, traders, businesses, and institutions to make confidential stablecoin transfers across multiple blockchain networks.

## Features

- **Wallet integration** — connect your existing wallet and start immediately
- **Browser-based encryption** — local, lightweight homomorphic encryption and ZK proof generation
- **Balance decryption** — view your encrypted balances privately in-browser
- **Multi-chain support** — confidential transfers across Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, and more

## No Overhead

Unlike traditional privacy systems, Stabletrust requires:

- No volatile privacy tokens
- No bridging to isolated chains
- No wallet switching
- Minimal encryption delays

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Visit [stabletrust.fairblock.network](https://stabletrust.fairblock.network) and connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, or any EVM wallet).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initial Deposit">
    Make a one-time deposit to set up your confidential account. This creates your encrypted balance on FairyRing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transfer">
    Send confidential transfers to any address. Amounts and balances are encrypted — only you and the recipient can see the values.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Selective Disclosure

Stabletrust supports selective disclosure for compliance. You can reveal specific transaction details to auditors or regulators without exposing your complete financial history.
